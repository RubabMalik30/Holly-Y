About
Holly Y
Felmlee

As a child, Holly zoomed around her neighborhood on her bicycle, chasing evil-doers or running from them, and always rescuing someone in distress. At sixteen, she wrote and directed a Christmas play, transcribed the diaries of a great-great-great grandfather who served in the Civil War, and wrote her first novel. Whew! No wonder she didn’t have any boyfriends.
